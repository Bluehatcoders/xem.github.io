Prettycode

may 2014

This is a small app (166b) made to display minified / golfed code in a reader-friendly way. (Thanks to @subzey and @aemkei for their help)

Source code: 
<input oninput=h="";for(i=99;--i;m?p.textContent=h+=v:0)m=1,v=value.replace(eval("/.{"+i+"}(?=(\\W=|.\\B.|..$)?).|.*/g"),function(v,l){m&=!l;return"\n"+v})><pre id=p>
